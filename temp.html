    chop

     verb
    verb
    NAmE//tʃɑp//
     
    Verb Forms present simple I / you / we / they chop
     
    he / she / it chops
     
    past simple chopped
     
    -ing form chopping

  1. enlarge image
    1to cut something into pieces with a sharp tool such as a knife chop something He was chopping logs for firewood. Add the finely chopped onions. chop something (up) (into something) Chop the carrots up into small pieces. (figurative) The country was chopped up into small administrative areas.
